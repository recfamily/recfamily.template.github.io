How will it be?

Who I Am

Image identification, using image reading / TAGs Bring a different user experience, allowing customized searches of objects, people and time in the images;

Possibility of eternalizing photos and moments between several generations Intelligent method of inheritance through existing members and families. Realize inheritance based on kinship, heirs and or family integration;

Through two family levels, (1 and 2 degree) it is possible to create a family directory tree. The junction between families maps all levels and distributions of families (see tree). Enables the inclusion and exclusion of family interactions and their members;

Cooperation between third-level members and families Members can contribute in their own family albums or in integrated families. Ranking of the best co-workers, readers and participants. Possibility to mark people in photos;
